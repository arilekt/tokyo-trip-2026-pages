📄 Sheet 5: Traveling to Accommodation & Emergencies

1. 🏨 APA Hotel Asakusa Taharamachi Ekimae

  • Nearest station: Tawaramachi (G18 - Ginza Line)
  • Exit: Exit 1 (There is an elevator) or Exit 2
  • walk: 1 minute (exit the station and immediately see the orange/black hotel sign)
  • Landmarks: Opposite Asakusa Post Office.
  • Map Code: 35.7106, 139.7913

2. 🏨 MYSTAYS Fuji Onsen Resort (Kawaguchiko)

  • Nearest station: Fujikyu-Highland Station (5 minute walk)
  • walk: Walk out of Fujikyu Highland Station, turn left and follow the main road for 400 meters, the hotel will be on your left.
  • Taxi: From Kawaguchiko Station it is about 1,000 yen (if you take the bus to Kawaguchiko Station, we recommend a taxi. The easiest)

3. 🏨 Shinjuku Granbell Hotel

  • Nearest station: Higashi-Shinjuku (E02/F12)
  • Exit: Exit A1 (There is an elevator)
  • walk: 4-5 minutes (turn left from A1, walk straight, see Inari Shrine, turn right into the alley)
  • Landmarks: Modern tall building with lights decorating the front of the hotel.
  • From Shinjuku Stn: Long walk (15 minutes), not recommended if you have large bags (Taxi ~1,000 yen)

4. ❄️ Palcall Tsumagoi Resort

  • travel: Hotel Shuttle Bus only (already reserved)
  • Pickup point: Karuizawa Station South Exit (go down the escalator and find the parking lot). Look for the bus that says "Palcall"
